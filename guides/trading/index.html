Trading

Master the marketplace and build your fortune through shrewd deals.

Trading is the economic backbone of Faceland. Whether you are selling crafted goods, rare materials, or hard-won dungeon loot, understanding the trading system lets you turn your efforts into wealth.

The Marketplace

The central marketplace is where players list items for sale and browse offers from others. It operates as a persistent auction-style system.

  • List items for sale at any marketplace kiosk in major towns
  • Set your own asking price or use the suggested market value
  • Listings remain active for 48 hours before expiring
  • Sold items deliver currency directly to your account
  • A small listing fee is charged to prevent spam listings

Check current market prices before listing to avoid underselling valuable items.

Direct Trading

For immediate transactions, players can trade directly with each other face to face.

  • Approach another player and send a trade request
  • Both parties place items and currency into the trade window
  • Both must confirm before the trade executes
  • A trade log records all transactions for dispute resolution
  • Direct trades have no listing fee but require both players to be present

Supply and Demand

Prices on the marketplace fluctuate based on player activity and game events.

  • Consumables like potions spike in price before major dungeon events
  • Crafting materials drop in value when new gathering areas are discovered
  • Rare drops from limited-time events command premium prices
  • Equipment prices vary based on the current meta and popular builds
  • Seasonal events often introduce exclusive tradeable cosmetics

Savvy traders buy low during quiet periods and sell high during peak demand.

Trading Perks

The trading skill levels up as you complete transactions, unlocking useful perks.

  • Reduced marketplace listing fees at higher trading levels
  • Access to bulk listing tools for selling large quantities
  • Ability to place buy orders for specific items you need
  • Expanded marketplace listing slots for more simultaneous sales
  • Reputation bonuses that attract more buyers to your listings

Building a reputation as a reliable trader attracts repeat customers.
