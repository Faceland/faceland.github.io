# Fishing
%Cast your line and reel in the catch of a lifetime.

Fishing is a relaxing yet rewarding profession in Faceland. Sit by any body of water, cast your line, and wait for a bite. The fish you catch can be cooked for powerful food buffs or sold for a tidy profit.

# Basic Fishing
Getting started with fishing is simple and requires minimal investment.

- Purchase a fishing rod from any tool vendor in town
- Stand near the edge of any body of water and cast your line
- Watch for the bobber to dip beneath the surface, then reel in
- Different water types contain different species of fish
- Your fishing level increases with each successful catch

%Patience is a fisherman's greatest virtue. Rare catches take time.

# Fish Species
Faceland's waters are home to a wide variety of fish, each with unique properties and uses.

- Sunscale Trout is common in rivers and a staple cooking ingredient
- Moonlit Bass appears in lakes during nighttime hours
- Ironjaw Pike inhabits deep underground waterways
- Glacial Salmon swims in the frozen northern streams
- Ember Koi is an extremely rare fish found near volcanic hot springs

# Fishing Perks
As your fishing skill grows, you gain access to perks that improve your catch rate and rewards.

- Quick Reflexes shortens the reaction window needed to reel in fish
- Deep Cast allows your line to reach deeper water where rarer fish swim
- Lucky Angler increases the chance of catching uncommon species
- Treasure Hunter gives a small probability of hooking treasure chests
- Master Fisher reduces the chance of your line snapping on large catches

%Combine fishing with cooking to create some of the best food buffs in the game.

# Fishing Hotspots
Certain locations are known for exceptional fishing. The crystal lake in the central highlands produces rare catches consistently, while the river delta near the coast offers high-volume fishing for traders. Explore different bodies of water to discover your favorite spots.
