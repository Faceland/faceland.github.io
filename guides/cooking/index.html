Cooking

Prepare hearty meals that fuel your adventures and warm your spirit.

Cooking transforms raw fish, meat, vegetables, and other ingredients into delicious meals that provide lasting stat buffs. A good meal before a dungeon run can boost your performance significantly.

Cooking Basics

Meals are prepared at a Cooking Fire or Kitchen Station using a recipe and the required ingredients.

  • Stand near a cooking fire and open the cooking interface
  • Select a recipe from your known recipes list
  • Provide the necessary ingredients from your inventory
  • Wait for the cooking timer to complete
  • Higher cooking skill unlocks better recipes with stronger buffs

You can build portable campfires to cook in the field during long expeditions.

Food Buffs

Cooked meals provide temporary buffs that last for a set duration. Only one food buff can be active at a time.

  • Hearty Stew increases maximum health by a flat amount
  • Grilled Fish Platter boosts critical hit chance and damage
  • Spiced Meat Skewers enhance strength and attack speed
  • Forest Salad improves gathering yield and movement speed
  • Golden Feast provides a balanced boost to all primary stats

Ingredient Sources

Cooking ingredients come from many different activities across Faceland.

  • Fish are caught through the fishing profession
  • Meat drops from animal enemies found throughout the world
  • Vegetables and fruits are collected through gathering
  • Spices are purchased from specialty vendors in distant towns
  • Rare ingredients are sometimes found in treasure chests

Combining ingredients from multiple professions often produces the strongest meals.

Cooking Mastery

At high cooking levels, you unlock the ability to prepare feasts that buff your entire party. These multi-serving meals are essential for guild events and dungeon groups. Mastering cooking makes you an invaluable asset to any team.
